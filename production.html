What is an Audio Publicist?


An Audio Publicist is a term I coined in 2017 to explain an aspect of what I do when it comes to my company Production Content, Inc. From my experience in working at a major record label and radio syndication company, press releases are a good way to communicate information to radio stations and audio news outlets. But I discovered that information is ingest, retained, and regurgitated if the messaging is in the form of a soundbite from the subject matter him/her self.
So, when it comes to working with an artist, record label, or any client that wants to convey information to an audio outlet, I have skills and experience to help you create a concise and effective message to audience you want to reach.
