Production Guru, really?


Let's start with this...audio projects I have produced over the last 2 plus decades have reached over 2 hundred million listeners in the US, Europe and Australia. I have produced radio programs in the rock, hip-hop, classic soul, Christian music and country music formats over the length of my career.
From 15 second commercials to 24 hour holiday programming, I have produced it all.
I have produced radio specials/programs for Kenny Chesney, The Allman Brothers, Carrie Underwood, Joey Ramone, Maren Morris, Roberta Flack, Alan Jackson, Trisha Yearwood, Brad Paisley, Garth Brooks, and many, many others.
Production pieces normally don't hit the record charts, but my work has done it twice. First with a special produced version of the song "To Life" from the legedary singer Eddy Arnold. Not only did the produced song chart, it made history. By hitting the charts in May of 2008, Eddy Arnold became the first (and still only) artist to mark 7 decades on the music charts.
My other production piece that hit the music charts is an all-star version of the classic holiday poem 'Twas The Night Before Christmas, which features an all-star combination of country music stars reciting the holiday classic. I first produced it in 2008, and since then, it has become a country radio tradition--one that other country music news outlets have gone on to emulate with their own versions.
